Rules of origin

If you are importing goods into the UK, you may be able to claim a preferential customs duty rate if the country from which the goods are coming has a Trade Agreement with the UK.

Select the country from which the goods are coming to identify if there are any applicable rules of origin that will impact your import.

The UK has trade agreements with these countries or trading blocs. Click on a country to find out more about the agreement and the associated rules of origin.
